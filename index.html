About Cenita

Cenita is a licensed company based in Rwanda, dedicated to providing exceptional travel and tourism experiences. Our mission is to showcase the beauty and culture of Rwanda through curated travel packages and tours. Our vision is to be the leading travel and tourism company in the region, known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Our Services

  • Hiking: Explore Rwanda's beautiful landscapes with our guided hiking tours.
  • Beach Party: Enjoy the sun and sand at our exclusive beach parties.
  • Games: Participate in fun and engaging games organized by our team.
  • Visit Rwanda: Discover the rich history and culture of Rwanda with our city tours.
  • Catering Services: Enjoy delicious meals prepared by our expert chefs during your trips.
  • Personal Itinerary: Get a personalized travel plan tailored to your preferences.
